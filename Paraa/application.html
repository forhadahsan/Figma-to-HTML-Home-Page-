Design Intern

About The Job

We're seeking a creative UI/UX Designer with 1+ years of experience to join our team. As a Designer, you'll play a key role in crafting intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces and experiences.

Responsibilities:
  • Design visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces for web and mobile applications
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand project requirements and user needs
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and design mockups to illustrate design concepts
  • Conduct user testing and gather feedback for iterative improvements.
  • Stay updated with industry trends and emerging design technologies.
Requirements:
  • Minimum of 1+ years of experience as a UI/UX Designer.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Sketch, Figma, or Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Strong understanding of user-centered design principles
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills..
  • Ability to translate complex concepts into simple and elegant designs..
  • A portfolio showcasing UI/UX design projects.
Skills Required:
  • UI/UX design tools (Sketch, Figma, Adobe Creative Suite).
  • Creativity, effective communication, collaboration, attention to detail.
  • Unlock your creativity with us! Shape intuitive interfaces, craft visually stunning experiences, and redefine user engagement.
